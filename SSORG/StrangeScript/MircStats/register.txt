mIRCStats Registration
======================
See also http://www.nic.fi/~mauvinen/mircstats/register.html



Some mIRCStats features require a license to fully activate them.

After you have made your registration you will receive 
your PERSONAL registration file by email within next 1-3 days. 

Just place that file (license.txt) into your mircstats directory and 
you can start using fully functional new features like:

** Personal stats (only available for registered users!)
     - Create personal sections with detailed data for top users

** Custom stats (only available for registered users!)
     - Create your own constantly changing stats sections!

** Chat Partners (only available for registered users!)
     - Find out who chats most with whom?

** Word stats (only available for registered users!)
     - Most used words - when those words were last used and by whom?
     - Lists nicknames who are mentioned in channel discussion most often.
     - Most used URLs in channel - shows them as direct links!

** Manual nick connection file
     - Specify which nicks actually belong to one person
     - Separate persons who use same irc client
     - You can use wildcards (* and ?) in nick filters
     - Link nicks to their homepages!
     - Use any IMAGE instead of nickname in user stats!

** Unlimited nick exclusion list!
     - Filter out channel bots and stats-abusing persons)

** Weekly and Hourly activity graphs 

** More Big Numbers sections

** Monthly activity calendars

** Highlight URLs as links in random quites, descriptions and topics.

** Use your small "Channel logo" instead of "@" to show channel operators or voiced nicks

** Compensate time zones by adding -+ hours to your log timestamps

** Unlock options which are only available for registered users (title styles etc..)

** You can shrink the mIRCStats info bar in the bottom of the page

** ...and a lot of other nice features!


This is a FULL LIFETIME REGISTRATION so you are 
entitled to use also all the future enhancements!
